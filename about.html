About Us

At Champions Global Consulting, we believe that everyone has the potential to succeed and make a positive impact on the world and we are committed to providing high quality services that are tailored to the specific needs of our clients. Whether you are an individual looking for personal development, a company seeking guidance on leadership and team building, or an organization looking to inspire an audience, CGC is the right place to come.

Our team, led by the founder and principal consultant, Dr. Ahmed Abdulkareem, is comprised of experienced individuals with knowledge and expertise in a variety of fields, including public speaking, coaching, mentoring, leadership, and organizational performance. We are dedicated to providing our clients with the support and guidance they need to succeed and helping them reach their full potential.

Guiding principles

  • All our services are customized to meet the specific needs of each individual and organization. We believe that the one-size-fits-all approaches do not work, and we will take the time to understand the specific goals and challenges of each client before developing a tailored plan.
  • We are committed to providing high-quality services that are based on the latest research and best practices. Our team members are experts in their fields, and they are continually learning and staying up to date on the latest developments in their areas of expertise.
  • We believe in the importance of open communication and collaboration. We will work closely with our clients to ensure that they are involved in the planning and execution of their plan, and that they have the information and support they need to succeed.
  • We are dedicated to the success of our clients. We will go above and beyond to help them achieve their goals, and we will provide ongoing support and guidance throughout the duration of our engagement.
  • We are committed to being transparent and accountable. We will provide clear, honest feedback and progress reports to our clients, and we will be open to feedback and suggestions from them as well.
  • We believe in the importance of diversity and inclusion. Our team is made up of individuals with a wide range of backgrounds and experiences, and we welcome clients from all walks of life.

  • We are dedicated to maintaining the highest ethical standards. We will always act with integrity and respect, including upholding confidentiality, and we will never engage in any practices that are unethical or harmful to our clients.

By following these principles, we are confident that we can provide our clients with the support and guidance they need to succeed. If you have any questions or concerns about our services, please do not hesitate to contact us. We look forward to working with you.
